Introduction

Customer feedback is an important aspect of any business. It can provide critical information about the health of a business, provide future product roadmap as well provide ideas for future innovations/sustenance of a business. In today’s world where brand image in social media can make or break a business, customer feedback can no longer be overlooked.

User Feedback

Feedback in general is considered as a reaction to a product or service, which in turn can be used as a basis for improvement. User feedback refers to the process of soliciting customer’s opinion about a certain product or a service. It attempts to gather the information if the customers were satisfied or dissatisfied with the overall experience. This information can be collected, aggregated, analyzed and turned into meaningful action items that a business can perform to improve the customer experience. At the core, user feedback is meant for improving future customer experience and eventually, to reduce churn.

Importance of User Feedback

  • Improve Customer Experience: Monitoring customer experience at every touchpoint is important. User Feedback can improve this monitoring by directly asking customers about their experience.
  • Improve Product & Services: Business can improve upon existing products and services by incorporating suggestions received from customers.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Customer sentiment can quickly be gathered by asking for their overall satisfaction. Customer satisfaction is closely tied to loyalty and plays a big role in the overall success of a business.
  • Social Marketing: Feedback from other peer users is considered to be the most reliable source of information by customers. Opinions provided by others customers can be made available by the business to establish trust factor with a new user.
  • Growth: In general User Feedback is critical to make an informed decision on the future of a business. Customer feedback is one of the most tangible sources of information to make business decisions.
